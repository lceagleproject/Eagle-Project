About The Project

At Goforth Elementary, behind the library, lies an outdoor garden area featuring two pergolas and picnic tables. Over time, the pergolas have become deteriorated, while the picnic tables and benches have shifted, rendering them dangerous and unusable. Situated adjacent to the main playground, the area should be a safe and inviting space for students, yet the disarray of the picnic tables and benches has made it less so.

The project will address these challenges by ripping out the hazardous and unusable picnic tables and benches. The pergolas will undergo thorough cleaning followed by sealing and staining. Under one of the pergolas, a gravel area with a picnic bench will be constructed. While the space under the other pergola will be transformed into a memorial area. A buddy bench will be placed toward the side of the area. A giant Chess board will also be created. A crushed stone walkway will connect both pergolas to the sidewalks.

This comprehensive effort will provide Goforth Elementary with a highly flexible outdoor learning environment, requiring minimal maintenance in the years ahead. Students, particularly those in the Chess Club, will benefit from a fun and engaging place to learn and practice. Furthermore, the buddy bench will serve as a fitting tribute to Mr. Healy, ensuring his legacy at the school he loves.

Any donation is much appreciated and will help us create an inviting environment where students can learn and socialize outdoors, fostering a sense of community and fun.
