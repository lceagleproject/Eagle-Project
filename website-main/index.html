About The Project

At Goforth Elementary, behind the library, lies an outdoor garden area featuring two pergolas and picnic tables. Over time, the pergolas have become stained and messy, while the picnic tables and benches have shifted, rendering them dangerous and unusable. Situated adjacent to the main playground, the area should be a safe and inviting space for students, yet the disarray of the picnic tables and benches has made it less so.

The project will address these challenges by ripping out the hazardous and unusable picnic tables and benches. The pergolas, although structurally sound, will undergo cleaning followed by sealing and staining. Under 1 of the pergolas a raised wooden deck will be built. Additionally, an ADA-compliant ramp will enhance the accessibility for all users. While the space under the other pergola will be transformed into a memorial area that will be filled with crushed stone. A buddy bench will be placed toward the side of the area. A giant Chess board will also be created from dark light paving stones with the addition of large Chess pieces for the board. A crushed stone walkway will connect both pergolas to the sidewalks.

This comprehensive effort will provide Goforth Elementary with a highly flexible outdoor learning environment, requiring minimal maintenance in the years ahead. Students, particularly those in the Chess Club, will benefit from a fun and engaging place to learn and practice. Furthermore, the buddy bench and Chess area will serve as a fitting tribute to Mr. Healy, ensuring his legacy at the school he loves.

Project Objectives

Outdoor Learning Area

We hope to create a safe, inviting and highly flexible outdoor learning environment for students.

Chess Club

We hope to help grow and help the newly formed Chess club by providing a space to practice and collaborate.

Paying Tribute

We hope to pay tribute to Mr. Healy and everything he has done for Goforth during his time at the school.
